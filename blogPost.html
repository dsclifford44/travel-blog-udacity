Bay of Fires

When you first catch a glimpse of the turquiose blue water and white sandy beaches you wonder if you are dreaming. But then it comes into view again and you realise you are not. The contrast of the blue sea, the white sand and the red lichen, covered rocks is stunning, truely breath taking. The red lichen growing on the rocks in this region is what gives the area it's name the Bay of Fires
